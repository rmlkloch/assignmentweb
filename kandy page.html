Kandy Travel Guide

Overview

Kandy, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, is known as the cultural capital of Sri Lanka. Nestled in the central highlands, it is famed for its scenic beauty, historical significance, and spiritual essence. The city is home to the sacred Temple of the Tooth Relic, one of the most important pilgrimage sites for Buddhists worldwide. Explore this guide to learn more about what Kandy has to offer!
