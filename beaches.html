Beaches & Coastal Areas of Sri Lanka

Explore the pristine beaches, stunning coastal landscapes, and serene waters of Sri Lanka.

Unawatuna Beach

Known for its golden sandy shore, crystal-clear water, and great diving spots, Unawatuna Beach is a tropical paradise.

Mirissa Beach

Famous for whale watching and its lively beach vibe, Mirissa offers stunning sunsets and a laid-back atmosphere.

Hikkaduwa Beach

A hotspot for surfers and snorkeling enthusiasts, Hikkaduwa is one of Sri Lanka’s most vibrant coastal destinations.
