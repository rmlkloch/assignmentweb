Colombo Travel Guide

Overview

Colombo is a vibrant city that combines modern urban culture with historical charm. The city is home to landmarks like the Gangaramaya Temple, and the scenic Galle Face Green, perfect for enjoying a sunset by the Indian Ocean.

Top attractions include the bustling Pettah Market, the National Museum, and the stunning architecture of the Independence Memorial Hall.

Explore this complete Colombo travel guide to discover more about what the city has to offer!
